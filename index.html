About Us

WarmBread Bakery is a boutique bakery based in London. We proudly create handcrafted bread that is organic and healthy. Our bread is made of premium ingredients and fresh seasonal flavours: spinach, carrots, berries, seeds, nuts — all healthy and fresh. WarmBread Bakery products are rich in protein, oils, nuts and fibres to keep you energised and healthy all day long.

Our Bread Philosophy

We believe that bread should be healthy. We want to change the traditional ways of eating bread by using local, fresh & organic ingredients in a better, more nutritious bread that's actually healthy. Our bread is 100% handicraft and includes veggies such as spinach, kale, sage, basil, nuts, whole grains and superfood ingredients.
